Sometimes I just want to find some activity that I can enjoy no matter where I am. However I do not always know what to do. Well, that is what google was made for, I thought several times. After some minutes of searching, most of the times I end up flooded in a bunch of browser tabs with many events that occured in the past and still bored with nothing to do.

Have you ever felt the same way? Ok, here is one place that can help us! In this place you will find events near you. Besides, after you score some of the events that you have attended, we can suggest you new things to do for the next time you get bored. Oh! and we will never suggest you events for which you'd need a time machine.

FAQ

I search this site, and I find nothing. Is it working?
We are now looking for supporters that tell us "Yeah! seems like a good idea, I would use it!". Like us on facebook and spread the word. If we get to our goal of 1,000 people backing us, we'll start building it for you.
Will I need to pay anything to use it?
No, if you are just looking fun things to do near you, you will not be charged for the use.
You told that you'll suggest me things based on other events that I attend, Will my privacy be in danger?
We are planning to use your information only to know which kind of things you like to do, and that way, we will be able to make you better suggestions the next time you want to have fun.
